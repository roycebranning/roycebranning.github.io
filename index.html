A man who was lame from birth...


lay at the temple gate called Beautiful, where he was put every day to beg from those going into the temple courts. When he saw two men about to enter, he asked them for money.

The two men looked straight at him. The one named Peter spoke, “Look at us!” So the lame man gave them his attention, expecting to get something from them. Peter continued, “Silver or gold I do not have, but what I do have I give you. In the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth, walk.”
